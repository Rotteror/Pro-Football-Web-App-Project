How it works

3 easy steps to participate in Pro Football challange

01

Register

After you create your account, you can start setting it up. Your account dashboard will guide you through all the most important steps to start participate in Pro Football Challenge.

02

Select your predictions

Every day you will have chance to predict the end of ten matches. Each correct prediction give you points for monthly Pro Football Challenge.

03

Submit

After you selection , you can submit your predictions for the current day and participate in monthly Pro Football Challenge. Every month three of you guys have chance to win 300 USD.
